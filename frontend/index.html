Capabilities
WHAT WE
ANALYSE
😶

Emotion Detection

DeepFace identifies 7 emotions — confidence, anxiety, neutral, happy, surprise, fear, disgust — from snapshots taken every 3-5 seconds.

DeepFace · CV
👁️

Gaze Tracking

MediaPipe iris landmarks track whether you're looking at the camera (simulating eye contact with the interviewer).

MediaPipe · 468 landmarks
🧍

Posture Analysis

Shoulder alignment, head tilt, and slouch detection via full-body pose estimation. Sit straight, score higher.

MediaPipe Pose
🎤

Speech Analytics

Words-per-minute, filler word detection (umm, like, basically), pause frequency, and vocal rhythm scoring.

Web Speech API · Librosa
🧠

Answer Quality

Claude evaluates content relevance, clarity, structure, and depth — scored on a 10-point scale per answer.

Claude · LLM
📋

Resume Intelligence

RAG-powered resume parsing extracts skills, projects, and experience — questions are tailored specifically to you.

LangChain · PyPDF2
